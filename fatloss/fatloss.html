Net Energy Expenditure Calculator

This weight loss calculator was made using formulas and algorithms developed after 10 years of working in the fitness industry. The calculations were made from algorithms developed by the extremely talented and intelligent, Milos Sarcev, IFBB pro bodybuilder and IFBB pro bodybuilding coach.

The algorithm is very precise and calculates net energy expenditure from an hour to hour basis. It is a tool to give a starting point, for one to estimate their net energy expenditure to create a diet which aligns with their fitness goals.
